## üéì Gig Optimization Tips

### Title Optimization
‚úÖ **Good**: "Build E-commerce Price Monitoring with Playwright & Alerts (3-14 days)"  
- Includes: What (Price Monitoring), How (Playwright), Benefit (Alerts), Timeline (3-14 days)

‚ùå **Bad**: "I will build you a Python app"  
- Too vague, no keywords, no timeline

### Description Optimization
**Structure**:
1. Hook (pain point)
2. Solution (what you'll build)
3. Features (bullet list)
4. Tech stack
5. Why choose me
6. Process
7. FAQ
8. CTA

**Length**: 800-1200 words (detailed but scannable)

### Gallery Optimization
- **1st image**: Hero shot (most important for clicks)
- **2nd image**: Key feature highlight
- **3rd image**: Another feature or workflow
- **4th-5th images**: Tech stack, testimonials, or process
- **GIF**: Show complete workflow

### Tag Optimization
Choose tags that:
- Buyers actually search for
- Match your core skills
- Aren't overly competitive
- Describe the deliverable, not just tech

## üêõ Common Publishing Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Mistakes
1. **Vague gig titles**: "I will build you a website"
2. **No screenshots**: Text-only gigs get <10% clicks
3. **Overpricing**: $500 for what others charge $200
4. **Underpricing**: $50 for what should be $500
5. **No FAQ**: Buyers have questions, answer them upfront
6. **No requirements**: You need info from buyers to start
7. **Too many revisions**: "Unlimited revisions" = scope creep
8. **Unrealistic delivery**: Promising 1 day for complex projects
9. **Copy-paste descriptions**: Generic descriptions don't convert
10. **No differentiation**: What makes you different from 1000 other devs?

### ‚úÖ Best Practices
1. **Specific titles**: Include keywords, tech stack, timeline
2. **Professional screenshots**: Clean, high-res, annotated
3. **Market-based pricing**: Research first, adjust later
4. **Clear packages**: Obvious value progression Basic ‚Üí Premium
5. **Comprehensive FAQ**: Answer objections before they ask
6. **Detailed requirements**: Know what you need upfront
7. **Limited revisions**: Scope protection (1-3 based on tier)
8. **Realistic timelines**: Under-promise, over-deliver
9. **Custom descriptions**: Tailored to each project
10. **Clear USP**: Fast response, quality code, platform protection

## üìû After First Message

When you get your first buyer inquiry:

### Respond Within 1 Hour
```
Hi [Buyer Name]!

Thank you for your interest in [Project Name]! I'd be happy to help you with [their request].

I have a few questions to ensure I understand your needs:
1. [Specific question about their requirements]
2. [Technical clarification]
3. [Timeline expectations]

Based on your answers, I can recommend the best package (Basic/Standard/Premium) and provide a detailed timeline with milestones.

I'm online now and can start immediately once we align on requirements. My response time is <1 hour during business hours.

Looking forward to working with you!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

### Convert Message to Order
1. **Ask qualifying questions** (requirements, budget, timeline)
2. **Recommend appropriate package** (match needs to tier)
3. **Send custom offer** (if needed, with exact scope)
4. **Provide quick estimate** (e.g., "Standard package, 7 days, $600")
5. **Answer all questions quickly** (<1 hour)
6. **Be professional but friendly**
7. **Close with CTA**: "Shall we proceed with [package name]?"
