## ðŸ“ˆ Pricing Psychology

### Anchoring Effect
- **Premium price** sets the anchor (makes Standard look reasonable)
- Most buyers choose **Standard** (70% of orders)
- Basic tier attracts budget buyers (20%), Premium for enterprise (10%)

### Package Differentiation

**Basic** (Entry Point):
- Core features only
- Fast delivery (3-5 days)
- 1 revision
- 7 days support
- **Price point**: $300-450
- **Target**: Solo developers, startups, first-time buyers

**Standard** (Sweet Spot):
- Enhanced features
- Moderate delivery (7-10 days)
- 2 revisions
- 14 days support
- **Price point**: $600-900
- **Target**: Small businesses, agencies, repeat buyers

**Premium** (Enterprise):
- Full features + integrations
- Longer delivery (10-14 days)
- 3 revisions
- 30 days support
- **Price point**: $1200-1600
- **Target**: Enterprises, high-budget projects, complex requirements

## ðŸŽ“ Pricing Tips from Top Sellers

### Start Conservative, Increase Later
1. **First 5-10 orders**: Price at market average (build reviews)
2. **After 10+ orders**: Increase by 10-20% (you have social proof)
3. **After 50+ orders**: Premium pricing (you're proven)
4. **Level 2 Seller**: Can charge 30-50% more than new sellers

### Test Different Price Points
- **Week 1-2**: Try lower end of range
- **Week 3-4**: Increase 10%, monitor conversion
- **Week 5+**: Find sweet spot where orders Ã— price is maximized

### Seasonal Adjustments
- **Black Friday**: 20-30% off (attract volume)
- **New Year**: "New Year special" promotions
- **Slow months**: Bundle deals (order 2+, get 10% off)
- **Busy months**: Premium tier only (maximize revenue)

## ðŸš€ Pricing Optimization After Launch

### Monitor These Metrics
- **Impressions**: How many people see your gig
- **Clicks**: Click-through rate (CTR)
- **Messages**: Inquiry rate
- **Orders**: Conversion rate
- **Revenue**: Total earnings

### Adjust Based On
- **High impressions, low clicks**: Improve title/thumbnail
- **High clicks, low orders**: Adjust pricing (may be too high)
- **Many Basic orders**: Upsell to Standard in messages
- **Few Premium orders**: Add more value or reduce price gap
- **Lots of orders**: Increase prices 10-20%
